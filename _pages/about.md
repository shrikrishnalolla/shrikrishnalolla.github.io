Hi!! I'm a Pre-Doctoral Fellow at the [IACV Lab](https://sites.google.com/iisc.ac.in/somabiswas/iacv-lab-iisc) in the Indian Institute of Science (IISc). I am advised by [Dr.Soma Biswas](https://sites.google.com/iisc.ac.in/somabiswas/home?authuser=0). My research interests lie in computer vision and generative models, such as Diffusion and Rectified Flows. Specifically, I've worked on machine unlearning and debiasing methods for image generation. 

Prior to IISc, I was a Software Engineer at Microsoft, working on improving the Windows OS Build System. I did my undergraduate in Electronics and Communications with a minor in Data Science from Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani - Hyderabad Campus.
